CRYSTAL COLLECTOR!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching the your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The total value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on the crystal.


Each time a new game starts both the random number and the value of each crystal will change.
